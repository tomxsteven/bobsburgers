The Beet-er Late Then Never Burger

This variation of The Beet-er Late Than Never Burger is made with a lamb shoulder, vodka pickled red beets, fresh beet greens, grilled vidalia onions, fried goat cheese (obviously), and golden beet mostarda on a potato bun.

Confronting the cold
Presence of the erratics
To nourish the weak

Served with fries, balsamic aioli, olive misto and a shot of fancy vodka.

Execution

  1. Sear patties and buns.
  2. Fry fries and goat cheese.
  3. Grill onions.
  4. Set plates.
  5. Put aioli and misto in ramekins.
  6. Pull buns.
  7. Spread mostarda on top and bottom buns.
  8. Flip patties and cover.
  9. Flip onions.
  10. Layer beet greens on the bottom bun.
  11. Check fries and goat cheese.
  12. Check patties.
  13. Put onions on the top bun.
  14. Season fries with oregano dust.
  15. Stack goat cheese on patties.
  16. Assemble burgers.
  17. Plate up.
  18. Order up.
