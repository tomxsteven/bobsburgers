The Beet-er Late Then Never Burger

This variation of the Beet-er Late Than Never Burger is made with an 80/20 beef patty, pickled red beets, fresh beet greens, grilled vidalia onions, fried goat cheese (obviously), and golden beet mostarda on a potato bun.

Confronting the cold
Presence of the erratics
To nourish the weak

Served with fries, balsamic aioli, olive misto and vodka.

Execution

  1. Sear patties and buns.
  2. Fry goat cheese and fries.
  3. Grill onions.
  4. Set plates and pull buns.
  5. Spread mostarda on top and bottom buns.
  6. Flip patties and cover.
  7. Flip onions.
  8. Layer beet greens on the bottom bun.
  9. Put aioli and misto in ramekins.
  10. Check fries and goat cheese.
  11. Check patties.
  12. Breathe.
  13. Put onions on the top bun.
  14. Season fries with salt and oregano dust.
  15. Stack goat cheese on patties.
  16. Assemble burgers.
  17. Plate.
  18. Order up.
