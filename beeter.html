Beet-er Late Then Never Burger

This variation of the Beet-er Late Than Never Burger is made with an 80/20 beef patty, pickled red beets, fresh beet greens, grilled vidalia onions, fried goat cheese (obviously), and golden beet mostarda on a brioche bun.

Confronting the cold
Presence of the erratics
To nourish the weak

Served with fries, balsamic aioli, olive misto and vodka.

Execution

  1. Sear burger and bun.
  2. Fry goat cheese and fries.
  3. Grill onions.
  4. Set plate and pull buns.
  5. Spread mostarda on top and bottom bun and plate.
  6. Flip burger and cover.
  7. Flip onions.
  8. Layer beet greens on bottom bun.
  9. Put aioli and misto in ramekins, then plate.
  10. Check fries and goat cheese.
  11. Check and rest burger.
  12. Breathe.
  13. Plate onions on top bun.
  14. Season fries with salt and oregano dust, then plate.
  15. Plate burger on beet greens.
  16. Plate goat cheese on burger.
  17. Skewer top bun set on goat cheese.
  18. Order up.
