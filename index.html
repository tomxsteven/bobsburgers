Tom's Bob's Burger Challenge

Objective

If you are curious about enhancing your improvisational cooking skills, this challenge should be a good start.

Produce six orders of the following burger combo within the allotted time.

  • Two hours to mise en place
  • Eight minutes to execute the six combos

  • Boss Mode:
    • Make the potato buns
    • And the mustard for the mostarda
    • Lol

The Beet-er Late Than Never Burger Combo

Golden Beet Mostarda
Basalmic Aioli
Vodka Pickled Red Beets
Lamb Patties
Fried Goat Cheese
Fries
Oregano Dust
Olive Misto
Misc. Vegetables
Potato Buns

These recipes do not include specific amounts or measurements. The instructions are intentionally vague to create a sense of familiarity and/or fear. Normally, my recipes are basic ingredients lists and doodles haphazardly scribbled on parchment paper. There is more than enough information provided to complete this challenge.

You'll need to incorporate your own style and techniques onto the structure of these recipes to produce a successful product. Think of this outline like a kitchen version of html.

If you fail, eat it your failure (don't waste food), and then try again.
If you complete the objective, you're a proper cook.
If you can teach someone else how to do it, you're a chef.

Letter to the Chefs:

There are solid methods in this outline, if you know where to look. You're just going to have to trust me.
